About me

I am a PhD student at Centre Inria de l’Université de Lille , within the FUN research team, in co-direction with the Antonine University in Lebanon. My work is jointly supervised by Nathalie Mitton and Carol Habib from France, as well as Jad Nassar and Joseph Asmar from Lebanon. My research focuses on the management of solar panels within a university campus using wireless sensor networks. In 2024, I obtained my Software Engineering and Networks degree from the Antonine University.
